TEssay Analytics Corporation

We use data science to optimize your college or graduate school application.

What we do at TEssay Analytics Corporation (TAC), after much distillation and extraction, is ultimately simple: We provide evidence-based answers to questions relevant to college and graduate school applications. And we do this all using the most powerful analytic techniques in the history of mankind: data science and machine learning.

Equipped with four years of professional experience, our own graduate experience in America, plus our knowledge and skills in data science and machine learning, we are here to help you optimize your application.

  • Essay Writing

    • By using the essay scoring system (Score My Essay) combined with our professional writing experience, we can provide more scientific and professional essay writing guidance to undergraduate and graduate applicants. Score My Essay was developed by our team of researchers using natural language processing to calculate word relevance and density of the personal essays of students admitted to top schools. Compared to traditional ways of writing, this system allows us to help undergraduate and graduate applicants improve the objective quality of their admissions essays.

    If you choose to DIY your own essay, you can still use Score My Essay to compare it with the essays that worked for top schools. It's free now!

  • Strategic School Selection

    • With the power of data science in Python  and admissions data, Strategic School Selection (aka Triple S) helps customers develop the objectively best application strategy: what schools to apply to, and how to improve your application. Our custom quantitative analysis is tailored to your personal profile and interests. It shows not only the big picture of multiple schools systematically, but also the relative application advantage and/or disadvantage of each school.

    • Undergraduate School Selection

      • This service helps undergraduate applicants select schools from the US News TOP 50. With the integration of ranking, admission rate, and tuition data, our own custom Application ROI index helps you understand which schools are actually better investments for your college application.

    • DVM School Selection

      • This service is specifically designed for students that are facing the extremely selective DVM admissions process. Our own custom  DVM Application Score helps you quantitatively combine up to 10 factors, and select schools that are more suited for you from all the accredited DVM programs across the world. Meanwhile, suggestions on how to leverage your advantages and prepare your application are provided. 

  • Personal Consulting

    • In our Personal Consulting service, we provide you with assistance tailored to your specific needs. For example, we can help you make objective decisions for your application through research and quantitative analysis. And we can provide guidance on how to prepare application materials.

    Finally, if there’s a service you need that is not listed above, again our Personal Consulting service is where we can work with you to specifically develop a custom service/product to address your unique problems.
