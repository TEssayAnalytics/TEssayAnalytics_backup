Strategic School Selection

TEssay Analytics


We are excited to announce a new family of TEssay Analytics services: Strategic School Selection (aka Triple S)!

These new analytics services leverage the power of data science  in Python and undergraduate/graduate admissions data  to help customers develop the objectively best application strategy: what schools to apply to, and what application factors to improve.

For many people, college or graduate school is one of the most important investments they will make in their life. This one decision, this investment can forever alter the course of your life. During the last 4 years at  TEssay Analytics working diligently and passionately with our customers as they navigate the application process, we found that the consulting industry for college or graduate school application is strongly lacking something crucial: a rigorous application strategy. Inspired by our previous experience with and love of mathematics, data science, programming, and general computer science, we decided to create a family of services to provide students with the evidence they need to make the objectively best decisions regarding their school selection.
