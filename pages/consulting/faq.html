Personal Consulting FAQ

1. What types of problems require the project-based consulting service?

To solve your problem(s), if it requires a continuous period of time, you will need a project-based consulting service. For example:

  • You need objective recommendations on which schools to apply
  • You need guidance throughout the preparation of various documents
  • You need guidance when you fill out and submit the application form

2. What kind of questions can I ask in the email-based consulting service?

The questions need to be very specific. One question can be about one kind of document, or factor (e.g. GPA and activities). For example, you can ask:

  • The GPA of my freshman year is very low, is there a way to make it look better?
  • My professor may not be able to submit the recommendation letter in time, what should I do?
  • Should I upload the transcript of the community college I attended last summer?

The following questions can only be answered through the project-based consulting:

  • Which school I should choose among these ten schools?
  • Can you help me check my application form and see if there are any errors?

3. Why do I need this service?

While there can be many reasons, it's often because you need objective recommendations based on data-driven research and analysis, and professional guidance on preparing your application.

4. How to purchase the Personal Consulting service?

Please refer to Request a Service.

5. Can I ask you questions that are outside the scope of the service I have purchased?

Questions beyond the scope of the service you have purchased can only be answered after you have purchased the corresponding service(s).
