Essay Writing FAQ

1. How many days after the payment will I receive the essays?

AFTER you complete the survey and provide all the information we need to write your essays, it takes 5 workdays to complete a personal statement. It takes 3 workdays to finish a resume. It takes 6 workdays to complete recommendation letters (2 or 3).

2. How long will it take to edit the essays?

After we receive your commented version, we will email you the edited version within 24 hours on workdays.

3. How many times do you edit the essays?

For each essay, we will edit it three times for free. After that, if you need further editing, we will charge you $50 each time.

4. How do I pay?

We accept AliPay, and WeChat payments. After you decide to purchase a service, we will email you the contract, which includes the payment information.

5. Which fields/majors have you helped students apply to?

We have essay writing experience in the following fields:

  • Computer science, electrical engineering, data science, and statistics
  • Economics, MBA, business analytics, finance, and public administration
  • Biology, veterinary medicine, and food science
  • Graphic design

6. Can I ask you questions that are outside the scope of the service I have purchased?

Questions beyond the scope of the service you have purchased can only be answered after you have purchased the corresponding service(s).
