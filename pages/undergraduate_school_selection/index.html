Undergraduate School Selection

TEssay Analytics


In the Undergraduate School Selection (aka USS) service, based on our own custom Application ROI index, we can help you understand which of the TOP 50 universities are actually better investments (and in some cases which are relatively worse) for your college application. This one index used together with ranking, tuition, or admission rate data, directly shows not only the big picture of all the schools systematically, but also the relative investment advantage and/or disadvantage of each school.

How does the USS service work?

USS service is provided to you in the form of a report. Upon purchasing the USS service, you will receive a report that contains the Application ROI and college admissions data, data visualization and analysis of the school tier you select. We divide the TOP 50 universities into 5 tiers based on ranking, and each report contains the data analysis of one tier:

  • Tier 1 Report: Admission data analysis on schools with rankings from 1-10
  • Tier 2 Report: Admission data analysis on schools with rankings from 11-20
  • Tier 3 Report: Admission data analysis on schools with rankings from 21-30
  • Tier 4 Report: Admission data analysis on schools with rankings from 31-40
  • Tier 5 Report: Admission data analysis on schools with rankings from 41-50

Price: 1615 Yuan (or 230 USD) per report

Note:

  1. The rankings are based on 2020 Best National University Rankings by US News.
  2. Since there are schools tied in rankings, every tier may contain more or less than 10 schools.
  3. College admissions data are from the fall 2020 entering class. They are the latest and most complete admission data.
