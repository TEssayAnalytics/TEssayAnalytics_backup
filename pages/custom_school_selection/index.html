Custom School Selection

TEssay Analytics


Through Custom School Selection (aka CSS), we provide application strategies that are specifically designed for your major/program.

Who can use the CSS service?

While the Undergraduate and DVM School Selection services are developed only for applicants of these two types of programs, CSS provides objective guidance on school selection to applicants of other majors/programs, such as Master in Data Science, Finance or Business Analytics.

How does the CSS service work?

CSS service is provided to you in the form of a report and personal consulting:

  • Upon purchasing the CSS service, you will be asked to provide relevant personal information and major/program/school interests.
  • After receiving your information, we will produce a report that contains schools we recommend and suggestions on how to prepare/improve your application. Additionally, the report shows you how the schools are selected quantitatively by presenting the raw data (admissions, programs, etc.), data visualization, and analysis of all the available schools.
  • In the end, through the personal consulting, we will answer your questions and help you make the final decision on which schools to apply to.
  • The whole service lasts 4 to 6 weeks.

Price: will be determined on a project-by-project basis.
